Sport is generally recognised as activities which are based in physical athleticism or physical dexterity
In organised sport records of performance are often kept and for popular sports this information may be widely announced or reported in sport news
The precise definition of what separates a sport from other leisure activities varies between sources with no universally agreed definition
There are opposing views on the necessity of competition as a defining element of a sport with almost all professional sport involving competition
A vegetable is an edible plant or part of a plant but usually excludes seeds and most sweet fruit
The non-biological definition of a vegetable is largely based on culinary and cultural tradition
A number of processed food items available on the market contain vegetable ingredients and can be referred to as vegetable derived products
Diets containing recommended amounts of fruits and vegetable may help lower the risk of heart diseases and type 2 diabetes
